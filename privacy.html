Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

🔒 Your Data Stays on Your Device

Smart EMI Loan Planner is a completely client-side web application. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. No loan information is transmitted to any server, database, analytics platform, or third-party service.

📦 What We Store

Item Storage Location
Loan History Browser localStorage
Language Preference Browser localStorage
Theme Preference Browser localStorage
Cookies Not Used
Analytics Not Used
Tracking Technologies Not Used
Advertisements Not Used

🗑️ Managing Your Data

You have complete control over your data.

  • • Use the "Clear All" option to remove saved loan records.
  • • Clear your browser storage to remove all application data.
  • • No account creation is required.
  • • No personal information is collected.

🌐 External Resources

The application may load publicly available frontend resources such as Tailwind CSS through CDN services. These resources are used only to render the application interface. No loan calculations or personal financial data are transmitted to those services.

⚖️ Disclaimer

All financial calculations provided by Smart EMI Loan Planner are estimates based on standard loan amortization formulas. Actual loan balances, prepayment amounts, foreclosure charges, and interest calculations may vary depending on lender policies and contractual terms. Always verify figures directly with your lender before making financial decisions.

✅ Privacy Commitment

This application is designed with a privacy-first approach. Your loan information remains on your device and under your control at all times.
