About Smart EMI Loan Planner

Smart EMI Loan Planner is a free, offline-first financial planning tool designed to help borrowers understand their active loans, remaining balance, prepayment opportunities, and potential interest savings.

🎯 What It Does

  • ✅ Calculate EMI for new loans
  • ✅ Calculate outstanding loan balance after paid EMIs
  • ✅ Analyze loan prepayment impact
  • ✅ Compare Reduce EMI vs Reduce Tenure strategies
  • ✅ Estimate loan closure amount
  • ✅ View amortization schedules
  • ✅ Save calculations locally in your browser
  • ✅ Export reports as TXT files

📊 Calculations Supported

EMI Calculation

Calculate monthly EMI based on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

Outstanding Balance

Determine remaining principal after a specific number of EMIs.

Prepayment Analysis

Estimate interest savings and loan reduction after making a lump-sum payment.

Loan Closure

Calculate the amount required to close the loan immediately.
